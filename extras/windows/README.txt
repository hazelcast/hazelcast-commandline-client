You can launch the CLC from the Hazelcast group in the Start menu.
Note that CLC tries to connect to a Hazelcast instance running locally on your computer - please update the configuration if your cluster is running elsewhere.

Useful links:

* Hazelcast CLC documentation: https://docs.hazelcast.com/hazelcast/latest-dev/clients/clc
* Get Started with Hazelcast: https://docs.hazelcast.com/hazelcast/latest/getting-started/get-started-cli
* Hazelcast CLC Survey - please complete the survey to share your feedback: https://forms.gle/RdCHAwYayqpcCgft8
